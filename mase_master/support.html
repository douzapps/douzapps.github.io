What are the different difficulty levels?

Maze Master offers four unique modes:

  • Easy: A smaller maze to learn the mechanics.
  • Medium: A balanced challenge.
  • Hard: For seasoned puzzle solvers.
  • Extreme: A massive, complex labyrinth that will push your spatial awareness to the limit!

*Progress is tracked independently for each difficulty level.

How is the score calculated?

You earn a base score depending on the difficulty you choose. Additionally, you receive bonus points for every second remaining on the timer when you reach the neon portal!

How do I change the game's language?

The game automatically detects your device's language. If supported, it will switch seamlessly. Maze Master supports 30 different languages.

How do themes work?

As you progress, the environment changes! A new visual theme (e.g., Ice, Fire, Earth) is automatically unlocked and applied every 5 levels you complete.

Does the game save my progress?

Yes! If you leave the app, your current level and score for each difficulty setting are automatically saved. You can always pick up exactly where you left off.
